About Myself

Hello! I'm an accomplished professional services consultant with a decade of experience, specializing in designing and deploying business process automation solutions for Fortune 500 enterprises. My journey has seen me wear multiple hats, from project manager and systems analyst to scrum master and technical lead. I've had the privilege of delivering innovative solutions to industry giants in Silicon Valley, Wall Street, and Hollywood. My expertise lies in driving digital transformation using AI/automation and strategic analytics. From streamlining production talent offer letters for Netflix to revamping vendor onboarding for Meta, my projects have consistently yielded significant cost savings and time efficiencies. Based in Venice Beach, I'm often referred to as an "Excel Wizard" and "Data Visualization Alchemist." Beyond the professional realm, I'm passionate about surfing, yoga, photography, and hiking with my rescue dog. I also have a deep appreciation for live electronic & hip-hop music and love to travel. My technical skills span across various programming languages, databases, cloud platforms, and SaaS tools. I'm always eager to connect and discuss how my expertise can help achieve your goals. Let's chat!
